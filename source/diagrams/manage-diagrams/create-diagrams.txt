===============
Create Diagrams
===============

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can create new diagrams in the :ref:`diagram view
<rm-diagram-view>` pane. To create a new diagram, click the plus
button on the right of the diagram tab. This creates and opens a blank
diagram. By default, the diagram view pane hides all entities in the
project.
