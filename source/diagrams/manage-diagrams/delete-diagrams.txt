===============
Delete Diagrams
===============

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can delete diagrams on the :guilabel:`Mapping` tab of Relational
Migrator. 

About this Task 
---------------

If you have at least two diagrams, you can delete diagrams in Relational
Migrator. Relational Migrator requires at least one diagram and by
default, creates the ``Main Diagram`` diagram with a Relational view and a
MongoDB view.

Steps 
-----

.. procedure::
   :style:  connected

   .. step:: Navigate to the diagram you want to delete

      Click the name of the diagram in the ribbon tab to display the
      diagram. 

   .. step:: Open the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      Click the arrow button on the right of your window to open the
      :guilabel:`Diagram Properties`pane. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram_properties.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties pane

      The :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane displays the active diagram name and
      notes. 

   .. step:: Click the ellipsis button in the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      This opens a menu of diagram options. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/duplicate_diagram.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties Options menu

   .. step:: Click the Delete Diagram option

      This deletes the active diagram.
