===============
Delete Diagrams
===============

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can delete diagrams on the :guilabel:`Mapping` tab of Relational
Migrator. 

About this Task 
---------------

- You must have at least two diagrams before you can delete a diagram
  because Relational Migrator requires at least one diagram in your project.

Steps 
-----

.. procedure::
   :style:  connected

   .. step:: Navigate to the diagram you want to delete

      Click the name of the diagram in the ribbon tab to display the
      diagram. 

   .. step:: Open the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      Click the :icon-fa5:`chevron-circle-right` arrow button on the
      right of your window to open the :guilabel:`Diagram
      Properties` pane. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram_properties.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties pane

      The :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane displays the active diagram name and
      notes. 

   .. step:: Click the :icon-fa5:`ellipsis-h` ellipsis button in the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      This opens a menu of diagram options. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-options.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties Options menu

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Delete Diagram` option

      This opens a confirmation box.  

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Delete Diagram` button in the confirmation box. 

      Once you confirm that you want to delete the selected diagram,
      Relational Migrator deletes the diagram. This step can not be
      undone.
